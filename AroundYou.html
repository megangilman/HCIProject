Color Around You

Color Overview

Using color in rooms can impact the experience of a person in that space. A lot of research has been done in this realm to uncover the phycological effects the colors present in a room has on someone. Research has uncovered trends in varying colors affecting both the physical and emotional state of someone. Furthermore reserarch has identified which colors are best implemented in different types of spaces. To learn more explore the Color in Spaces page.

Color in Spaces

Color in spaces is a design element that can influence the mood and perception of a space. The color of a room can affect the way people feel and behave. For example, warm colors like red, orange, and yellow can create a cozy and intimate atmosphere, while cool colors like blue and green can create a calm and relaxing environment. Color can also be used to define different areas within a space, create focal points, and highlight architectural features.
