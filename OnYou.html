Color on You

Color Overview

Color theory has been studied in depth for years as the field that analyzes how colors work together to affect a viewer's emotional response and perceptions. Color is leveraged everyday in projects, homes, businesses, clothes, toys, and much more. Color is all around YOU and ON YOU! Color on you can affect the way that you are perceived along with the way your individual beauty shows through.

Identifying your color season is done through the process of analyzing the undertone of your features and how they align against each other. To complete your own color analysis you can visit href="https://colorwise.me/self-analysis" >Colorwise.me o complete an online assessment. There are four main color seasons and three subseasons for each, which further narrows down your cues. To explore more visit the Color Season page.

Using color in rooms can impact the experience of a person in that space. A lot of research has been done in this realm to uncover the phycological effects the colors present in a room has on someone. Research has uncovered trends in varying colors affecting both the physical and emotional state of someone. Furthermore reserarch has identified which colors are best implemented in different types of spaces. To learn more explore the Color in Spaces page.

Red

Red is a color that is associated with energy, war, danger, strength, power, determination as well as passion, desire, and love.

White

Symbolizes purity, cleansing and a new beginning.Color of balance and purity.

Pink

Tied in with femininity, feeling of love and romance. Men who wear pink exude confidence in their masculinity and femininity.

Grey

Wearing gray can push elegance, simplicity and stability. Can give people a sense of realism. However it can also symbolize loneliness and indifference.

Orange

Signals new possibilities, creativity, warmth, ambition, good times, and positivity.

Golden

Associated with royalty and wealth.

Green

Triggers a calming and healing reaction. Is associated with generosity.

Blue

Blue is a soothing and calm color. Is associated with a calming, soothing loyal feeling.

Black

Signals a feeling of depth and mystery along with darkness.
